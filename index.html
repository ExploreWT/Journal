Welcome to Explore Journal

Dive into the world of science.
Study complex scientific articles through our simple text analysis tools.
New Explore Journal issues are published every week.

What are we helpful for?

1

Change the website theme using buttons in the navigation menu on the top of a page.

You can choose one of the styles depending on your mood or a topic of the article that you are currently reading. Otherwise you can use neutral default style settings so nothing is bothering you from studying. It is up to you to decide!

We prepared for you 5 themes:

  1. Default. Minimalistic design - simple color set and no extra details.
  2. 1500-1800 time span. Imagine reading an early print book. Enjoy Renaissance period.
  3. First half of XX century. Feel the chaos of Cubism.
  4. Late XX Century / start of XXI Century.
  5. 2030, future.

2

Go to Issues to start exploration.

Select from the list of Issues the one that interests you. EXPLORE button on the top right of the page will open for you our text analysis tools and navigate you to the exploration journey. The section gives to a reader the following possibilities:

  • to read the articles (with full screen one article display)
  • to compare the articles (with default three articles simultaneous display)
  • to study the articles with key words search
  • to study the articles statistics
